FairFlow Guardian - 2 Minute Video Script
Target runtime: 2:00
Voiceover word count: 155

Slide 1 (0:00-0:15)
FairFlow Guardian is an AI trading safety layer for the BGA track. It proves when a market is fair enough to review, and when no trade is the healthiest action.

Slide 2 (0:15-0:34)
The retail problem is not only prediction. A trade can look profitable and still be unfair because of hidden costs, thin liquidity, spoofing, leverage pressure, or opaque automation.

Slide 3 (0:34-0:54)
FairFlow turns public market data into liquidity, volatility, cost, manipulation, and risk features. The strategy agent can propose, but execution requires fairness, risk, route, and audit gates.

Slide 4 (0:54-1:18)
In the demo, start with the Competition Runway: readiness, audit hash, evidence pack, route proof, and calm paper approval. Then switch to Manipulated and show the refusal.

Slide 5 (1:18-1:41)
The best proof is the manipulated case. Same system, same asset, lower market integrity. FairFlow blocks the trade, locks the route, and records why.

Slide 6 (1:41-2:00)
Close on the judging criteria: retail fairness, agentic depth, risk-first design, and inspectable proof. Better systems, not bigger bets.
